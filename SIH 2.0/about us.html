About FakeTrace

At FakeTrace, our mission is to empower users to verify the authenticity of digital content, from photos to videos, ensuring that what you see is real.

Our Mission

FakeTrace was founded with one goal in mind: to help individuals, organizations, and media outlets detect and combat deepfake technology. As deepfakes become increasingly sophisticated, distinguishing between real and altered media has become a critical challenge. By providing cutting-edge detection tools, we enable people to verify the authenticity of media in seconds.

How We Detect Deepfakes

  • Image & Video Analysis: Our algorithms analyze visual inconsistencies in photos and videos using deep learning models like CNNs and RNNs.
